Solomon Kin Trust Architecture

Institutional Integrity.

The Kin Financial OS is built on a non-custodial framework. We provide the intelligence and the shield, while your capital remains exactly where it belongs: in your insured accounts.

🛡️

Non-Custodial Shield

Kin Financial never "holds" your money. We use secure API bridges (Plaid/Yodlee) to direct your routine while your funds stay FDIC-insured.

🔑

Bank-Level Encryption

All data moving through the OS is protected by AES-256 bit encryption—the same standard used by the world's largest financial institutions.

📱

Biometric Guard

Access to the Cockpit and Nexus Vault requires Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) including fingerprint or facial recognition on mobile devices.

The "Blanket" Technology

1. Permissioned Access

You authorize the OS to view your balance and obligations via secure bank-linkage.

2. Virtual Partitioning

The OS "shields" your bill money in the Nexus Vault using virtual ledgers.

3. Automated Fulfillment

The OS pays your bills directly from the shielded pool, preventing late fees and overdrafts.
