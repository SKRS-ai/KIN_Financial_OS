The Kinship Covenant.

This agreement outlines our commitment to your financial autonomy and the rules of the Kin Financial Operating System ecosystem.

1. Non-Custodial Nature

Solomon Kin, LLC provides a software interface ("The OS") for managing third-party financial accounts. We do not maintain custody of funds, nor do we act as a traditional banking institution. All funds remain in the user's linked FDIC-insured accounts.

In Plain English:

We provide the tools and the "Shield," but your money stays in your bank. We never touch your actual cash.

2. The "Blanket" Automation

By enabling "Shielding" or "The Blanket," users grant the OS limited power to initiate transfers between linked accounts for the purpose of bill fulfillment and debt mitigation as defined by the user's settings.

In Plain English:

When you turn on automation, you're giving the OS permission to move your bill money to your Vault so you don't accidentally spend it.

3. Kinship Pipeline (Minors)

Guardian accounts are legally responsible for all activity within Kid Kin and Teen Kin sub-accounts. Solomon Kin, LLC adheres strictly to COPPA regulations regarding the data of users under age 13.

In Plain English:

Parents are the bosses. You are responsible for what your kids do on their versions of the OS.

By using the Kin Financial OS, you agree to the Solomon Kin, LLC standards of financial integrity and community solidarity.
