## 🛡️ The Vision: "Financial Clarity Through Automation"
Most banking apps tell you what you *did*. **KIN-PFOS** tells you what you *can do*. 

By sitting as a non-custodial layer between a user’s bank and their financial obligations (utilities, rent, subscriptions), we provide a secure "Blanket" that protects the primary checking account. Our goal is to eliminate financial anxiety through **Projection** and **Precision**.

---

## 🧒 The Kinship Activity Pipeline (Life-Stage Banking)
We have engineered a multi-generational pipeline to ensure a user never has to leave the Kin ecosystem.

1. **Kid Kin Account:** The "Toy Bank" depot. A sandbox environment for ages 5–12 to learn the basics of goal-setting with full Guardian oversight.
2. **Teen Kin Account:** Controlled independence for ages 13–17. Focuses on "Safe-to-Spend" training and real-time parent monitoring.
3. **College Kin Account:** The bridge to adulthood (ages 18–22). Specialized for managing grants, tuition, and the transition to a professional salary.
4. **Adult PFOS:** The full Financial Operating System. Features 1-Click Bill Pay, Credit Shielding, and the physical/virtual **Nexus Blanket Card**.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Security Protocol
* **Core:** HTML5, CSS3 (Stadium-Grade UI), and JavaScript.
* **Integrations:** Plaid API (Banking Data) & UtilityAPI (Direct Utility Sync).
* **Security:** 256-bit bank-level encryption. 
* **Model:** Non-Custodial. We provide the "Operating System"; the user's deposits remain in their preferred FDIC-insured bank.
