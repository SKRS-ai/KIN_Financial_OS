Your Data, Your Property

Our Privacy Covenant.

At Kin Financial, managed by Solomon Kin, LLC, we believe your financial history is a private asset. We use your data to power your OS automation—never to sell to advertisers.

No Data Selling

We do not, and will never, sell your personal or financial data to third-party marketing firms. Our revenue comes from OS subscriptions, not your privacy.

Encryption at Rest

All sensitive financial identifiers are encrypted before they even reach our servers, ensuring that your "Blanket" remains invisible to the outside world.

The Kinship Shield

Kid and Teen Kin data is subject to enhanced COPPA-compliant protections. No behavioral profiles are ever built on minor users.

Your Data Rights

Right to Portability
